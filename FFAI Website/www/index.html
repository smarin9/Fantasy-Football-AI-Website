FantasyFootball A.I.

Welcome to Fantasy Football AI

For our group project, we designed and implemented an agent that will advise fantasy football players on how to set their fantasy lineup each week. Fantasy football is a popular game, where people who participate in fantasy football leagues draft individual NFL players who they think will earn the most fantasy points each week. The players also have the option to trade any player of theirs with a player that was not drafted by anyone in the league on the free agent's list. When a fantasy player drafts his or her team, they draft more players than they can start each week. Those extra players occupy the bench spots. The bench consists of players on your team while the free agent list has players, not on anyone’s team. Each week, coinciding with the NFL schedule, users select 9 players that they believe will have the highest amount of combined fantasy points form their entire team. Our agent uses available NFL statistics to predict the number of points each NFL player on the fantasy team will earn along with the predicted points of the top 50 free agents. The fantasy points are awarded based on the individual's performance in each NFL game. Our group is interested in this topic because each of us knows at least one person who cares deeply about his/her fantasy team or has a team of our own. Every participant would like to see his/her team succeed. Most people compete against friends or family for fun, but for some, money is involved. Our agent would do the research for them, giving them a leg up on their competition. If we can make our agent more accurate than the ones the user’s fantasy league prediction sites, like ESPN, already provide, we would be more reliable than the competitors.
